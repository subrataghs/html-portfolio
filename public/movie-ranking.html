The Best Movies According To Subrata

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

This is my favorite fantasy romantic drama movie of all time. The best part is the middle part of the movie, where they live together. I wish I could live like this one day. :-)

The Shawshank Redemption

One of the best Crime Thriller movie I have ever seen.

Intersteller

This is the best sci-fi adventure movie by the marvelous director Christopher Nolan. I almost don't like sci-fi space movies, but this one blew my mind.
